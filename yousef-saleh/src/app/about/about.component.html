Our Story
Yousef Saleh Yousef Cont. & Est. was founded in 2007 with a focus on achieving management goals and growing steadily. Over the years, the company has successfully completed nearly 50 projects, ranging from residential units to large industrial and commercial complexes. This success has been achieved consistently across all projects, regardless of size. As a result, the company has earned a reputation as a reliable and result-oriented firm in the Kingdom. Yousef Saleh Yousef has expanded its business within the construction sector, working with prominent clients such as Saudi Electricity Co., Royal Commission Jubail, Alawal Bank, and many others. The company has also served numerous individuals, further establishing its strong presence in the market.
