Our Story
Yousef Saleh Yousef Contracting and Establishment (YSY) was established in 2007 with a clear vision: to build a legacy of excellence in the construction sector. With over 17 years of experience, YSY has successfully completed over 50 diverse projects, ranging from residential homes to largescale industrial and commercial complexes. Our strength lies in our dedication to quality, innovation, and a customer-centric approach. From our humble beginnings, we have grown into a trusted name in the Kingdom’s construction industry, continuously pushing the boundaries of what is possible in modern construction. At YSY, our leadership plays a crucial role in shaping our success. Under the guidance of our esteemed Chairman, Yousef Saleh Al Dossary, and Project Director, Shamsuddin Khan, we have built a strong foundation for delivering projects that exceed client expectations.
Completed nearly 50 projects across various sectors.
Provided services to a large number of individuals.
Projects include residential, industrial, and commercial complexes.
Gained reputation as a result-oriented company in the Kingdom.
